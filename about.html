Where restraint
becomes radiance.

Maison Veil was born from a simple, radical belief: that the most powerful luxury speaks softly. Founded in Hyderabad in 2019 by Aryan and Meera Veil, the brand grew from a small atelier into one of India's most respected voices in considered contemporary fashion.

Every collection is a meditation on restraint — on the courage it takes to say less, to subtract instead of add, to trust in the quiet authority of exceptional fabric and precise cut.

We do not follow trends. We believe that the most enduring pieces are those that transcend them entirely. Each garment is designed to be lived in, not just worn — to age with its owner, accumulating meaning with every use.

Our Principles

01
Radical Restraint

We remove rather than add. Every design decision is interrogated until only what is truly necessary remains. The result is garments of unusual calm and lasting power.

02
Material Honesty

We use only natural fibres from traceable, artisan sources. The texture, weight, and drape of our fabrics are themselves a form of expression — chosen not merely for beauty, but for truth.

03
Artisan Legacy

Every piece involves the hands of skilled craftspeople, many from families who have practiced their craft for generations. Their knowledge is woven into every seam.

Our Commitment

Making fashion
that doesn't cost the earth.

Sustainability is not a marketing strategy for us — it is the only way we know how to make clothes. We use deadstock fabrics, low-impact dyes, and zero-plastic packaging. Our atelier runs on solar energy, and we plant a tree for every order.

Our Story

2019
The First Atelier

Maison Veil opens its first studio in Banjara Hills, Hyderabad with a team of six artisans and a debut collection of 12 pieces.

2020
Vogue India Recognition

Featured in Vogue India's "The New Luxury" editorial. The Fog Linen Coat sells out in 48 hours, establishing the brand's national presence.

2022
Flagship in Mumbai

First retail flagship opens in Mumbai's Colaba neighbourhood, designed by architect Noor Patel as a space of "lived minimalism."

2025
Global Shipping Launch

Maison Veil begins shipping to 45 countries. The brand becomes the first Indian luxury label to be stocked at Dover Street Market, London.

2026
AW '26 Collection

Our most ambitious collection to date — a meditation on shadow, weight, and the beauty of the incomplete.
